Why you should adopt:

There are many reasons to adopt a shelter pet, here are just a few. First, you are giving an animal a second chance at a happy life. You would be helping the pet overpopulation problem, and making room in the shelter for more animals in need. This also helps contribute to stopping cruel mass breeding facilities. Many don’t know that a lot of shelter animals are already house trained, especially the adult dogs. And many shelters have low adoption fees. Adopting a shelter pet also encourages others to adopt from shelters when they see how wonderful your pet is. Having a pet can help you maintain an active lifestyle and can provide physical and mental health benefits. So, if you are ready to adopt, check out your local shelters first!
